# Advanced Topics

The remaining sections (VI through XI) are not required for a good general understanding. They are specialization tracks that let you go deeper into specific aspects of ancient history.

- VI. Economy, Society, and Power
- VII. Warfare, Technology, and Administration
- VIII. Late Antiquity (300–700 CE)
- IX. Comparative Empires & Global Systems
- X. Working with Ancient Sources
- XI. Research & Writing
